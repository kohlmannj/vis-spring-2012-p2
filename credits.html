Madison Commons Article Explorer (Prototype)

About

The Madison Commons Article Explorer (Prototype) allows Madison Commons readers to explore the world of community events, issues and activities through a beautiful geographical visualization. Each circle on the map represents an article, colored according to which regional topics it pertains to. Readers can explore the rich local content of Madison Commons literally without losing their place in the world.

Instructions

  • Movement: Click and drag on the map to pan around the visualization.
  • Zooming: Use the (+) and (-) buttons in the upper left corner or double-click on the map.
  • Explore Articles: Hover over an article to display its title, an excerpt and its tagged topics.
  • Read Articles: Click on an article to read it without leaving the visualization. Use the Close button in the upper-left to continue exploring.

Data

The data used in this visualization was retrieved on April 24, 2012 from Madison Commons. This includes the Madison Neighborhoods KML map seen on the site, which Article Explorer uses to position articles on the map.
